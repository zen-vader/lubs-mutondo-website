Company Overview

One contractor, seven disciplines under one roof.

Lubs Mutondo Limited is a duly registered private company, limited by shares in accordance with the laws of Zambia, with its offices in Lusaka. The company has established itself as a contractor and supplier of high quality, reputable brands of automotive equipment, accessories and reagents, and other general goods and services in the country.

Since incorporation, we've supplied goods and services spanning events management, construction services & materials, office equipment, equipment installation among several others — built around a core specialty in electronic security: the supply, installation and maintenance of CCTV, intruder alarms, electric fencing, access control, biometric scanners, intercoms, gate automation and vehicle tracking.
